Dr. Verghese Kurien

The Father of White Revolution


The Time Line of Dr. Verghese Kurien

  • 1921 - Born in Kozhikode, Kerala

  • 1940 - Graduated in Physics from Loyala College, Madras(Chennai)

  • 1946 - Formed The Kaira District Cooperative Milk Producers' Union Limited(KDCMPU) soon known as 'Amul Diary' along with Tribhuvandas Patel.

  • 1948 - Earned Master of Science in Mechanical Engineering from Michigan State University on government Scholarship

  • 1949 - Became an officer of Dairy Division for five years at Anand, Gujrat

  • 1950 - Took over Amul Diary

  • 1963 - Received Ramon Magasay Award

  • 1965 - Became the Chairman of National Diary Development Board(NDDB) created by Lal Bahadur Shastri

  • 1965 - Received Padma Shri

  • 1966 - Received Padma Bhushan

  • 1970 - NDDB launched 'Operation Flood' also reffered as White Revolution

  • 1973 - Founded Gujrat Co-operative Milk Marketing Federation Ltd.(GCMMF)

  • 1979 - He founded the Institute of Rural Management(IRMA)

  • 1989 - Received World Food Prize

  • 1999 - Received Padma Vibhushan

  • 2005 - His Autobiography 'I too had a Dream' as told to Gouri Salvi

  • 2012 - Dies at the age of 90


  • November 26 - Celebrated as National Milk Day in India every year on his Birthday.
